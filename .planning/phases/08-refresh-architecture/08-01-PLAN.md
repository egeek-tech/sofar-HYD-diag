Purpose: The backend becomes purely reactive -- it never initiates reads on its own. All reads are triggered by browser WebSocket messages. This directly addresses REFR-01 (no autonomous backend refresh) and REL-03 (consistent inter-read delay by eliminating timer-driven timing).

Output: Refactored backend with timer-free sections, context-cancellable streaming reads, new `read_cycle` message type, PackInfoProbes skip logic, and updated tests.
